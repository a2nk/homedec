The V 4 House By Studio Mk 27. The low bunker-style home is a bold mix of tropical furnishings with ultra-modern architecture. The sprawling ground-floor house has a slab roof supported by a series of columns.

With each new project contemporary architecture is forced to optimize spaces and use the maximum of each square meter of the site. The two bedrooms are contained behind large perforated wooden panels, shading them. It is a contemporary house located in São Paulo, Brazil.
